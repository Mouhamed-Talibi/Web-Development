Book Title : Atomic Habits

Review: :

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear attempts to break down the science of habits and show you how small changes can lead to big results. The central idea is that tiny habits can be more powerful than big goals. Clear works to provide a framework for creating good habits and breaking bad ones that centers around building systems rather than relying on motivation or self-discipline. The framework centers around the "Four Laws of Behavior Change", which are to make habits obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying. By implementing strategies that align with these "laws," Clear says you can overcome common obstacles and make lasting changes in your life.

Review:

Review:
